Do I have grounds to evict?

Grounds For Eviction

  • Tenant is behind on rent
  • Violating the rental agreement
  • A crime has been commited on the property
  • Housing addition occupants not on the lease
  • Refusing to renew lease under same terms and conditions
  • Not allowing landlord entry into the property
  • Owner wishes to occupy property
  • Removal of the rental market
  • Sell of property

No Grounds For Eviction

  • Breach of the rental agreement to provide a habitable premises
  • Deducting needed repairs from the rent
  • Landlords refusal to accept the rent
  • Retaliatory eviction
  • Discrimination eviction
  • Defective notice from landlord
